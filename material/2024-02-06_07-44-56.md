While I didn't have the pleasure of this aphorism being expressed to me directly, the sentiment is clear. The woman who said it - from the deep south, woman of color, grandmother, amongst the wisest women I'd ever met - used this aphorism to describe what my mother had even printed on a mug in her classroom: 

Do not teach a pig to sing, it wastes the time and annoys the pig. 

They rightly mean different things...
